How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your personal data that we have collected via our app, please follow the steps below:

  1. Contact us at gamesgalaxy011@gmail.com with your request.
  2. Include your username or email associated with the app.
  3. We will process your deletion request within 30 days.
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

If you have any questions, please contact us.
